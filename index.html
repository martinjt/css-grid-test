About us

Welcome to the OpenTelemetry in Practice series

This is a new experiment by some OpenTelemetry contributors in the End User Working Group. We’re aiming to address practical problems that commonly stop development teams from succeeding with OpenTelemetry. We’re also hoping to build stronger connections with developers focused on specific languages. Finally, we want to improve the experience of implementing OpenTelemetry in production. Each OpenTelemetry in Practice will include half an hour of lightning talks and a half hour of open conversation about the topic.
